"Discrete mathematics is the branch of mathematics that
studies countable or distinct structures. It includes topics such as:
Combinations: The selection of items from a larger set.
Graphs: Mathematical structures used to model pairwise relations between objects.
Logical statements: The study of propositions and their truth values.
Discrete mathematics is essential in computer science, cryptography, and combinatorial optimization,
as it deals with structures that can assume only distinct values, contrasting with continuous mathematics.
